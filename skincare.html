Don’t Skip SPF

✔️ Regardless of routine, always finish with broad-spectrum SPF 30+ every morning to prevent aging, pigmentation, and maximize results of your serums.

✔️ Apply from lightest to heaviest consistency: hyaluronic acid → anti-aging serum → retinol (night) → SPF (day).

✔️ Patch‑test new serums—especially retinoids and acids—to avoid irritation.

✔️ Consistency matters: noticeable results usually take 4–8 weeks.
