DIY Glass Skin Routine: How to Achieve Radiant, Dewy Skin in 7 Days

Craving that poreless, luminous glow known as glass skin? This Korean beauty trend is all about intense hydration layered through a clean skincare routine. With the right steps and top-rated Amazon products, you can start seeing results in just one week.

✨ What Is Glass Skin?

Glass skin is a complexion that's smooth, clear, highly hydrated and almost reflective—like glass. It’s not about heavy makeup; it’s about healthy, glowing skin from within.

📋 Step-by-Step Routine

1. Double Cleanse

Start with an oil-based cleanser to remove makeup and sunscreen, followed by a water-based cleanser to wash away impurities.
