Hair Loss Treatment: What Works According to Dermatologists

Real solutions that actually work — backed by science and beauty experts.

Hair thinning is a growing concern across all age groups. From stress to genetics, the causes are many—but thankfully, dermatologists have identified effective treatments. This blog explores the most recommended products and methods to stop hair fall and encourage regrowth.

🔍 What Causes Hair Loss?

Common causes include:

  • Genetic predisposition (Androgenetic Alopecia)
  • Hormonal changes (PCOS, postpartum, menopause)
  • Nutritional deficiencies (Iron, Biotin, Vitamin D)
  • Stress and poor scalp health
  • Harsh hair products and tight hairstyles

💆‍♀️ Lifestyle Tips

  • Do daily scalp massages to increase circulation
  • Switch to silk pillowcases to reduce breakage

🧪 Advanced Treatments

If topical treatments aren’t enough, dermatologists may suggest advanced therapies like:

  • PRP (Platelet-Rich Plasma) injections
  • Low-Level Laser Therapy (LLLT)
  • Microneedling with growth factor serums
