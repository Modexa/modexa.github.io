My Happy Routine (With a Toddler in Tow 👶)

As a mom of a bubbly 2-year-old who still gets mistaken for being younger, my days start early and are filled with tiny adventures. From breakfast prep to bedtime cuddles, I've created a rhythm that keeps both of us smiling and sane!

🌞 Morning Start

My day begins with warm lemon water (detox magic!) and a 10-minute stretch on my non-slip yoga mat. While my little one plays nearby, I sneak in a few pages from a motivational book that keeps my spirit high.

🍳 Breakfast & Prep

A smoothie in my portable blender gives me energy on the go. I prep toddler snacks and lunches using colorful, sectioned containers that make meals feel fun and easy.

🧘‍♀️ Midday Me-Time

Nap time = my time! Whether it’s a quick walk with ankle weights or lighting a lavender candle, I refresh my mind.

💡 Tips I Swear By:

✨ Smart Planner: Use a daily self-care planner to track water, meals, movement & moods.
🕓 Time Blocks: Assign 30-minute focus blocks for chores or self-time. A smart watch helps me track it without checking my phone.
🍵 Calming Evenings: End your day with an herbal tea sampler and a short gratitude journal entry. It works wonders!
