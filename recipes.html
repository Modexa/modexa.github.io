Banana Oat Pancake Bites

⏱️ 15 minutes

Mini, mess-free, and sweetened naturally — these pancake bites are a toddler’s favorite!

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1/2 cup quick oats
  • 1 egg
  • Optional: cinnamon, mini chocolate chips, blueberries

Steps:

  1. Mix banana, oats, and egg together.
  2. Add extras, if using, and mix well.
  3. Spoon onto hot pan and cook both sides.

Veggie Cheese Wraps

⏱️ 10 minutes

Colorful, quick, and healthy — these wraps are lunchbox lifesavers!

Ingredients:

  • 2 tortilla wraps
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese
  • 1/4 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/4 cup cucumber slices
  • 1 tbsp hummus or mayo

Steps:

  1. Spread hummus or mayo on wraps.
  2. Layer veggies and cheese.
  3. Roll tightly and cut in half.

Cheesy Garlic Toasts

⏱️ 12 minutes

Need a quick crowd-pleaser? This garlic toast is golden, gooey and fast!

Ingredients:

  • 4 bread slices
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese
  • Optional: chili flakes

Steps:

  1. Mix garlic with butter and spread on bread.
  2. Top with cheese and bake or toast until golden.

One-Pot Chicken Pasta

⏱️ 25 minutes

Minimal cleanup, maximum comfort! This creamy pasta is ready in a single pot.

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts (cubed)
  • 2 cups pasta
  • 1 cup milk + 1 cup water
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese

Steps:

  1. Cook chicken with garlic, paprika, salt & pepper.
  2. Add pasta, milk, and water. Simmer till done.
  3. Stir in cheese and serve warm.

No-Bake Chocolate Oat Bars

⏱️ 15 minutes (+ chilling)

Quick and chewy with no oven required — perfect snack bars for all ages!

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 cups rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips

Steps:

  1. Melt peanut butter and honey.
  2. Stir in oats and press into tray.
  3. Top with chocolate and chill.
